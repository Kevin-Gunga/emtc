about us

Exiera Medical Training College is a dedicated institution in Kenya, specializing in the training of healthcare professionals, including courses for Health Care Assistants and various other medical-related programs. The college focuses on providing quality education and practical skills necessary for students to excel in the healthcare sector. Exiera aims to equip its graduates with the competencies needed to meet the growing demand for healthcare services in the region. Through its comprehensive curriculum and hands-on training approach, Exiera Medical Training College plays a crucial role in shaping the future of healthcare in Kenya.
